# Getting Started

## Prerequisites
- Mid-level infrastructure experience (2-10 years). The target audience is DevOps engineers, SREs, and platform engineers.
- A terminal, git, and an AWS account (free tier is sufficient for most templates)
- Working knowledge of infrastructure-as-code concepts, containers, and CI/CD pipelines

## Your First Decision (15 Minutes)
1. Open the README.md and go to **Quick Start**
2. Answer the 4 questions about your team
3. Follow the recommendation to the relevant decision tree
4. Read the full tree for your situation
5. Clone the matching template from `templates/`

## Time Commitment
- 15 minutes: Your first decision (Quick Start)
- 1-2 hours: Deep dive on a single decision tree + deploy a template
- 4-6 hours: Work through the entire kit
