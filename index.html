Why is Kubernetes last?

Because K8s is for scale, and most of you don’t need it. Serverless handles 90% of use cases more simply. Learn K8s first, you use it for everything. Learn serverless first, you use K8s only when it’s the right call. That distinction is what hiring managers test for.
